ArTech

ArTech is an educational platform created to support self-taught and unsupervised artists who struggle to navigate the overwhelming amount of art resources available online. Many artists learn independently, relying on scattered tutorials and references, often leading to confusion, slow progress, or burnout. ArTech aims to solve this by providing structured, research-based guidance that simplifies the learning process.
